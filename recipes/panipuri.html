How to make Panipuri the easy way?

Ingredients

  1. water
  2. pudina
  3. coriander
  4. salt
  5. puris
  6. tamarind paste

description: make sure you have all the ingredients in mentioned quantity

steps to follow to make delicious panipuri

  1. pour water in a container
  2. pour crushed pudina, coriander, chilli paste inside the water and let it soak for 23 hours
  3. add tamarind paste
