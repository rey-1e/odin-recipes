ingredients

  • rice batter
  • salt
  • water
  • edible vegetable oil

steps to follow to make delicious dosa at home:

  1. add 1 tsp of edible vegetable oil on a heated pan
  2. pour one ounce of batter over the oil and gently swirl it so that it takes shape of a solid circle
  3. add some more oil on the top of the dosa, and gently remove the dosa from periphery after it seems heated

your delicious dosa is ready to be served!
